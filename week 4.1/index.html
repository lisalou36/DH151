Toxicity in California Streams, 2015
Hover over a dot to see:
  • the name of the Station,
  • the year the sample was taken,
  • the stream's toxocity category,
  • the categorization of the land use,
  • and the percent of urban land used within a 5 km radius.

Samples' toxicity levels are designated as:

  • Non-Toxic
  • Some Toxicity = significantly different from control using TST
  • Moderately Toxic = having had one highly toxic sample in five years
  • Highly Toxic = significantly different and having a response less than the high toxicity threshold of 38.6% survival.

Land use qualifications are defined as:

  • "urban" if they had more than 25% urban spread within 5 km
  • "agricultural" if they had more than 50% spread of cultivated crops
  • "other" if neither urban nor agricultural
