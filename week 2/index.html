Southwest Roadtrip through the Elements
Favorite Places

Petrified Forest National Park

Arches National Park

Bonneville Salt Flats

Zion National Park

Valley of Fire State Park

These five sites were my favorites in a two week long roadtrip I took with a friend. This is one of the most memorable trips I have ever been on, not only because of the gorgeous locations, but also because we experienced a completely bonkers weather whiplash! We saw extreme heat, rain, snow, and hurricane level winds all within a few days of each other. I loved it.
